// THE 5 RULES THAT MAKE OR BREAK THIS PLAN

01Earn in dollars as fast as possible. Every year in naira-only income is a year lost to inflation. This is the single highest-leverage move available to you.
02Save before you spend. Move money to investments on payday, not what's left at month end. What's left is always zero.
03Never sell your index funds. The S&P 500 has returned ~10% annually for 100 years. Your job is to keep buying and never panic-sell.
04Skills are your highest-return asset right now. A ₦30,000 course that gets you a $2,000/month raise returns 800% in year one. Invest in yourself first.
05Avoid lifestyle inflation. Every raise should increase your investment rate, not your rent or car budget. The wealthy live below their means longer than anyone expects.
